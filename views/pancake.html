Homemade Pancakes


A recipe for homemade pancakes that doesn't require pancake mix!

Materials:

  • 2 Bananas
  • 2 Eggs
  • Mixing Bowl
  • Pure Vanilla Extract
  • Fork or Whisk

Procedures:

  1. Place 2 bananas in the mixing bowl and crush them with the fork.
  2. Crack the eggs open and add them to the mixing bowl and mix.
  3. Add a teaspoon of vanilla extract and mix again.
  4. Add a teaspoon of cinnamon and mix again.
  5. Place the batter into a pan heated on low to medium heat make the batter on the pan into a circle with the spatula.
  6. Remove the pancakes after they are done (darkened and solid.)
  7. Be sure to serve warm, and enjoy!
