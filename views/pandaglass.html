Panda Glass


A glass with a cute panda face on it, for a niece, nephew, or any other child!

Materials:

  • Printout of the following template: tinyurl.com/panda-printout
  • Glass
  • Scissors
  • Tape
  • Sharpie
  • Hankerchief(optional)

Procedures:

  1. Wipe off any smudge marks on the glass using the handkerchief (optional).
  2. Roughly cut out the panda face.
  3. Tape the panda face from inside the glass, pointing the face outwards.
  4. Add a teaspoon of cinnamon and mix again.
  5. Trace the black parts of the panda face using the sharpie on the outside of the glass.
  6. Remove the panda face.
