Homemade Air Freshener


A homemade air freshener to make your house smell like a home!

Materials:

  • Jar
  • Baking Soda
  • Essential Oil
  • Paper
  • Scissors
  • Mechanical pencil
  • Tape

Procedure:

  1. Unscrew the lid of the jar.
  2. Fill up the jar with baking soda.
  3. Pour about 10 drops of essential oil.
  4. Close the jar and shake well.
  5. Remove the lid and trace it onto a sheet of paper.
  6. Cut out the traced lid.
  7. Tape the paper onto the jar.
  8. Poke holes through the paper using the mechanical pencil.
