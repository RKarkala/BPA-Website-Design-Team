Amazon

Many people who are a part of our audience (young adults, teenagers, etc.) are often more inclined to buy a product off the internet rather than go to a store to buy a single item. This is why Amazon is important to our cause, as many times, when attempting to complete a DIY project, one may be missing only 1 or 2 basic supplies or ingredients. In this scenario, it may be compelling to simply quit on the project itself rather than go to the trouble of visiting a local store to buy the item. Amazon provides another option for someone in this scenario, and allows them to continue with minimal setbacks.
