Tom Thumb

Tom Thumb is a well known and respected supermarket chain with 2,205+ stores in the U.S. They are most well known for their grocery departments, as most of their sales are in food items. This store is definitely a go to for anything related to foods, however they do have a wedding department and run a pharmacy. Although the last two specifications seem to not apply to DIY projects, it is good to know what is available at each store you plan to buy at. Tom Thumb also maintains low prices and high quality throughout their stores, and are often consistent in their products from store to store.
